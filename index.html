About Text Enhancer Tool

The Text Enhancer Tool is powered by ChatGPT and designed to improve your writing by providing functionalities like translation, key information highlighting, readability checking, and tone adjustments. Try it out! If you have experienced any difficulties or negativities, please send an email to rex.zhang@emory.edu or louis.lu@emory.edu. Our technical staff are friendly and professional!

Data Privacy

We respect your privacy and are strongly committed to keeping secure any information we obtain from you. We will not collect and store any of your data when you use our tool except for processnig your text input to generate responses.
